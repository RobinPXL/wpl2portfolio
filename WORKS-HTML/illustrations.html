Illustration projects

Ever since I was 10 years old, I drew all kinds of things. I have a strong affinity with creatures of all kinds. My illustrations focus on color, rendering, composition and detail work. This is only a small sample, the best place to check out my works would be on Instagram.

Soaring High

I really tried to focus on the lighting and the shading of this piece. The part I'm most proud of is the implementation of 2 light sources that don't fight for each other's attention.

Originally I wanted to make it in an Aztec like art work, but because I was traveling at the time I had no reference to base it off of. I didn't want to lose any time and just continued working with natural colors and really focus on the lighting here.

Root: Might, Right and ...Arson!

This boardgame captured my heart and soul! I spent my entire summer of 2022 entirely engrossed in it. Of course, I wanted to draw all the creatures. This inspired me and a lot of people on Reddit for me to continue to illustrate these woodland creatures in multiple ways.

This game had captured me for the entire summer of 2022. I had an absolute blast making fan-art for both the official board game: Root the game of Might and RIght, but also the podcast that played TTRP called: Root, a game of Might, Right and ... Arson! I listened to three episodes and I really like their characters. I drew every single one and put them all in one big illustration/banner.

Spotify Albums

Ever since I got my spotify account 2 years ago, I've been creating playlists left and right. Sometimes it's difficult to find a specific playlist if they overlapping artists. That's why I made my own 'album' covers to make it easier to find.

The inspiration and motivation to create my own spotify album/playlist covers was by my all time favourite artist: Kyle Ferrin. Kyle Ferrin is a board game illustrator and his specialty lies in drawing doodly creatures. He mostly uses ink and bright popping colors. I thought I should also draw a cover within my own specialties. I love night scenes and woodland animals, but my style has that purple magical flair to it.

Still life: la familia café

I had to get to a dentist appointment once and I was a bit too early. So I decided to grab a coffee in a nearby alley. That's how I found this vvery cosy café near the Avenue de Volxem, Forest.

I sat down and began drawing a table in front of me. It had some delicious croissants in front of it and I thought that it would be a fun challenge to draw it. Looking through my brushes, I wanted to experiment with tools that I haven't used much before. I had a lot of fun using the 'Nico Bull' brush.
