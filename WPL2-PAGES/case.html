WPL2 case

Electronica - ICT rebranding

In the second semester of my first year, I have a course called 'Werkplekleren 2', abbreviated as WPL2. This course builds upon a similar first-year course, WPL1. Its aim is to apply our learning to a semester-long project, encompassing the creation of a brand guide, a functional website, a prototyped application, a campaign/marketing plan, and a final pitch & demo presentation for the project.

hello

Intro

Our client, PXL University College, oversees the Electronics-ICT cluster. This cluster is often misunderstood as focusing solely on circuit boards and mechanics, when in fact it covers a wide range of 'smart' technologies and teaches practices relevant to current industries, including Internet of Things, Cloud Computing, Machine Learning and AI, Cybersecurity, Digital Twins, and more.

This projected started on 26th of February 2024 and ended on 28th of May 2024.

Work process

We used Agile Scrum to organise our project. Additionally, we used Jira and Confluence as tools to keep track of the workload and timeschedules.

This project went through 4 sprints with each sprint having a clear goal: research & discovery, structuring & preparation, Creation and Refinement
